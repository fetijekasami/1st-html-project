History of computers: A brief timeline

The history of computers began with primitive designs in the early 19 th century and went on to change the world during the 20 th century.

The history of computers goes back over 200 years. At first theorized by mathematicians and entrepreneurs, during the 19 th century mechanical calculating machines were designed and built to solve the increasingly complex number-crunching challenges.

The advancement of technology enabled ever more-complex computers by the early 20 th century, and computers became larger and more powerful.
